KuCoin Bot Guide

How to configure a Spot Grid bot on KuCoin Web and sync it to the Dashboard

1 Open Trading Bots

Log into your KuCoin account on your desktop browser. From the top navigation menu, hover over the Trade menu and select Trading Bot.

Hover over Trade and click Trading Bot.

2 Select Spot Grid

On the trading terminal, look at the right-side panel under the Bot tab. Select Spot Grid to begin configuring your bot.

Choose Spot Grid for volatile markets.

3 Enter Custom Parameters

Switch to the Custom tab. Here, you will configure the bot according to your chosen EQTY/USDT Strategy (A, B, C, D, or E).

  • Low / High: Enter the bottom and top boundaries of your price range.
  • No. of Grids: Enter the number of grids for your strategy.
  • Total Investment: Enter the amount of USDT you want to allocate.

Click the Create button to launch your bot.

Fill in the Low, High, Grids, and Investment fields.

4 View Your Running Bot

Once your bot is created, scroll down below the chart to the Trading Algorithm panel. Click the Running Bots tab to view your active bot.

Your bot will appear in the Trading Algorithm list.

5 Copy Bot Data

To sync this bot with the community dashboard, you need to copy its data. Click and drag your mouse cursor over the entire row of your running bot to highlight all the text, then press Ctrl+C (or Cmd+C on Mac) to copy it.

Important: Make sure you highlight from the pair name (e.g., EQTY/USDT) all the way across to the APR percentage on the far right.
Highlight the entire horizontal row and copy it.

6 Paste into the Dashboard

Open the EQTY Grid Monitor dashboard and click the Add Bot button. In the modal that appears:

  • Type your handle (e.g., @zolnode).
  • Click inside the KuCoin bot snapshot text box and press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V) to paste the text you copied.

The dashboard's smart parser will instantly format the text vertically and extract all your metrics automatically.

Paste the text. It will format vertically and be ready to submit.
